How it works in practice

A frictionless journey from scanning a code to getting served.

1

Scan & Join

Customer scans a QR code at your venue to instantly view the virtual queue.

2

Get Ticket

They select their desired service and receive a digital ticket with position.

3

Enjoy Wait

Customers are free to roam, grab a coffee, and track their status live via web.

4

Get Served

An automatic notification is sent when it's their turn to approach the counter.
